About Us

Rizo Tech is a family-run refrigeration company built by owners who are still in the field. We specialize exclusively in frozen beverage dispensers and ice machines for high-volume, multi-location operations.

We do not chase small one-off repairs. Our work supports enterprise contracts with 100+ machines where uptime, accountability, and performance matter.

We built this company after years inside corporate service organizations where undertrained technicians and constant callbacks were the norm. Our difference is simple: the owners are the technicians. We solve the problem, verify the fix, and stand behind the work.
